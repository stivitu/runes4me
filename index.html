About the Oracle


The runes are equivalent of egyptian Toth tarot and as such they express quality of the moment. Shortly meditate on situation you need help for, then cast runes. It is not productive to cast runes every few minutes. As our life changes from minute to minute, so result will never be the same. Respect to the matter is crucial. Outcome should not be taken as ultimate thruth, then as directios for possible action connected to asked question. When comunicating with Great spirit(or Universe) if you like, question asked has to be clear.
So, have fun, ad I wish you luck in all your further achievements!

Outcome


First three runes are representing, in order, past, present and future. The past represents things which are in process of ending. Present represents things which are allredy moving, future represents things that are about to come. The fourth rune, result, represents a sort of sum, or solution for the situation.It is to use if outcome is not quite clear.Blue color of rune background represents reversed rune and its meaning is different.
